Recreation

It's good to be a hard working person, but it's alo good to take a little break one in a while and relax your mind. There are a few ways I do this.

One of the most obvious ways is to spend time with some friends. I think that's the way most people my age relax these days. It's always a great thing to have some people to spend time with and feel like there's not a care in the world. It really doesn't matter what we do. We could be playing a game of football or sitting on the sofa. They both work the same. I'm always in the mood to make new friends. I know that they can come from anywhere and I want friends from everywhere. But I think that some of my greatest friends have to be my family. No one can ever care as much and give me the push I need like they do. Sometimes I play a nice table game to take my mind off of everything else. Pool, ping pong, golf, video games, just something to take my mind off of the world for a moment.

...And if those don't work, I take a nap.
