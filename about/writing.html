Writing

So I guess it's pretty plain to see that computers and programming haven't always been the love of my life, but writing is something that has simply always felt good to do.

I remember writing my first story when I was seven. I don't know why, but i just decided to take a pencil and paper and write whatever was on my mind. Somehow that turned into a story. There really was no plot to it, but a story no less.

Today, I continue writing, but I don't just write stories. I also write scripts, poems, and everything in between. Some of my work has even been published. This is my own little uniqueness and I cherish it.
