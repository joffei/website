Mardise

"You are my rock"
That's what she says to me.
But she couldn't be further from the truth.

I am more like an assembly of pebbles
each tangentially connected to one another,
ready to crumble under the pressure
of the wind that surrounds it.

I am full of holes head to toe,
this sinner,
this one that doesn't deserve
the amazing person his God has sent him,
but I will cherish this beautiful gift
because I know I won't get another.

She compares me to pure elements,
but I see many imperfections in this body.
I tell her about the cracks in my concrete
but she tells me she can she clear enough through these crevasses
to see the greatness that lies within.

She says I'm a rock but a rock is strong,
I am weak at the knees from the very sight of her,
but when these pebbles break apart,
I will link them together to give her the beautiful pearl necklace she wants.
I just hope this imperfect body can give her something beautiful.

This imperfect body she calls a rock,
so wrong,
but her soft heart is able to fill these holes
and seal these cracks within me.
please don't leave me.

P.S. You complete me.
